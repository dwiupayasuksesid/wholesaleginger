About Us

Dwi Upaya Sukses LLC is an agricultural products’ trade company focusing in spices and tropical plants like peppercorns, ginger, and coffee beans. Our Indonesia-based LLC is established in 2021 and started as a local tradesman and middleman for farmers who cooperate with us on said products.

In order to meet with the increasing demand and quality of said products while introducing premium products from Indonesia to international market, our LLC begins the market expansion in 2025.

Vision
To become a leading company in Indonesia for its high quality production and distribution of spices to international market.

Mission
1. Empower local farmers with sustainable partnerships.
2. Supply natural and high-quality spice products.
3. Build an extensive and reliable supply chain to various countries.
4. Maintain an efficient, transparent and sustainable supply chain.


Featured Products
1. Black & White Peppercorns
- Originated from Sumatera and Kalimantan
- Sun-dried with moisture content <12% (in accordance to export requirements)
- Suitable for culinary and herbal medicine use

2. Ginger
- Originated from Central and East Java
- Available in large-sized and small-sized varieties
- Fresh dried for culinary and herbal medicine use

3. Coffee Beans (Arabica & Robusta)
- Originated from Sumatera, Java, and Sulawesi
- Green beans grade, sorted for export
- Have a bold, earthy, and unique floral taste

Company Values
1. Quality: High-standard product selection and processing.
2. Trust: Integrity in trade relations and partnerships.
3. Sustainability: Environmentally friendly agricultural practices.
4. Connectivity: Opening global market access for local farmers.

Market and Expansion
After serving the domestic market since 2021, Dwi Upaya Sukses LLC has expanded to international market in 2025, targeting countries in East Asia, the Middle East, and Western Europe. We continue to build distribution networks, logistics, and export legality, including Phytosanitary Certification, HACCP, and V-Legal.
